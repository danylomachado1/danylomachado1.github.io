Developed a script chain to test and calibrate photomultipliers for the Mobile Antineutrino Demonstrator (MAD) project and conducted a background study for ROADSTR during an underground deployment. Also developed a method to estimate specific activities for natural radioactive isotopes, including **40K**, **235U**, and **232Th**.
